Overview

WES TEK operates westek.co and its related site The Wastelander (wastelander.app) as informational websites designed for English-speaking users in Canada and the United States. Our websites do not require user accounts or logins.

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under Canadian law (PIPEDA) and California law (CPRA). We are not targeting EU/UK users, so GDPR/EEA rules do not apply. This policy is written in clear, straightforward language consistent with privacy principles.

Key Points:

• We collect only necessary information to run our websites and respond to inquiries.

• We do not sell or share your personal information for marketing purposes.

• You have rights to access, correct, and delete your personal information.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our websites, we automatically log technical data to secure and improve our sites:

  • IP address and general location information.
  • Browser type and version.
  • Operating system.
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website.
  • Referring website addresses.
  • Device information (mobile, desktop, tablet).

Analytics Data

We use Rybbit for analytics, which records anonymous visit data including:

  • Page URLs visited.
  • Session duration.
  • Referrer information.
  • Device and browser type.
  • Visitor's country or region.

Important: Rybbit does not store your IP address or use cookies, protecting your privacy.

Information You Provide

If you submit our contact form, you voluntarily provide:

  • Your name and email address.
  • Inquiry type and message content.
  • Any files you choose to attach (handled by Uploadcare).
  • App version, device model, and iOS version (for bug reports).

Bot Protection Data

We use Botpoison for spam protection, which collects:

  • IP address.
  • Browser and operating system information.
  • Form activity patterns.

What We Don't Collect: We do not request or collect sensitive information such as government IDs, financial data, health information, or special category data.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for legitimate purposes related to our services:

  • Contact Form Data: To respond to your inquiries, feedback, or support requests.
  • Visit Data: To analyze traffic patterns and improve our websites.
  • Technical Data: For security purposes, including preventing attacks and fraud.
  • Bot Protection Data: Solely to prevent spam and automated abuse.

We do not:

  • Use personal data for marketing purposes.
  • Share or sell your information for advertising.
  • Use automated decision-making or profiling.

All personal information is used solely to provide and secure our services and communicate with you. This limited collection aligns with the purposes explained when the data was collected.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to support site functionality and analytics. A cookie is a small text file stored by your browser that helps remember preferences and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Functional Cookies: Remember form fields or site settings to improve your experience.
  • Security Cookies: Help protect against fraud and maintain site security.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Privacy-First Analytics: Our analytics provider Rybbit does not use cookies at all – it tracks visits in a cookieless way to protect your privacy.

Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate our websites. Each acts as a data processor on our behalf:

Formspark

Purpose: Form Submissions

Processes contact forms and collects only the fields you enter. Formspark explicitly states it does not sell your data.

Botpoison

Purpose: Bot Brotection

Prevents spam and automated abuse by analyzing form submissions. Botpoison will never sell or rent your data.

Uploadcare

Purpose: File Uploads

Manages file attachments you submit. Uploadcare does not sell or rent your personal data.

Rybbit

Purpose: Analytics

Provides site usage analytics with aggregated, non-identifying data. Does not collect personal information or sell data.

All service providers process data solely to deliver their service to us. They have privacy policies confirming they use data only as instructed and do not sell personal information.

Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

  • Contact Form Submissions: Retained to allow us to respond and follow up, then deleted when no longer needed.
  • Technical and Analytics Data: Stored only for the time required to maintain and improve the site, then purged.
  • Security Logs: Retained for up to one year for security and performance purposes.

In accordance with PIPEDA's retention principle, we do not keep personal information longer than necessary. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

International Data Transfers

Although our users are primarily in Canada and the U.S., some service providers may transfer data internationally:

  • Formspark and Botpoison: Operate servers in Europe (Ireland).
  • Uploadcare: U.S.-based company with global infrastructure.

By using our forms, you consent to these transfers. We rely on our service providers' legal safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) to protect your data. These transfers are necessary for providing the services described above.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure:

  • All data is encrypted during transmission using SSL/TLS.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • We use industry-standard security tools and practices.
  • Regular security assessments help identify and address vulnerabilities.

Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach involving your personal information:

  • We will promptly notify affected users (typically by email within 7 business days).
  • We will report the incident to authorities as required by law.
  • We will take immediate steps to secure our systems and prevent future breaches.

However, no system is perfectly secure. We encourage you to contact us if you have any security concerns.

Your Privacy Rights

Under privacy laws, you have certain rights regarding your personal information:

If you are a Canadian Resident (PIPEDA):

  • Access your personal information we hold.
  • Correct inaccuracies in your data.
  • Know how we use your information.
  • Withdraw consent to processing (subject to legal exceptions).
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

If you are a California Resident (CPRA):

  • Request disclosure of categories and specific personal data we collect.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Opt-out of sale (though we do not sell personal data).
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below. We will verify your identity and respond within the legal timeframe.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our websites are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13 years of age.

If we learn that we have inadvertently gathered a child's data, we will promptly delete it. Parents or guardians should not submit personal information for children under 13 through our sites.

No Automated Decision-Making

We do not use personal data for automated decision-making or profiling. All decisions involving your personal information are made manually by our staff as needed (for example, responding to a support request).

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will post any revised policy on our websites and indicate the effective date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
